Muscular System

The endurance and power behind athletic activities is a result of training and nutrition, but also from the type of muscle fibers that make up the athlete’s physique. Genetic expression in the muscular system alters fiber type, recovery, and other aspects of muscle physiology. Muscle type and physiology varies greatly between different different levels of athleticism.
