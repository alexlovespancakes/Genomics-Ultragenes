Cardiovascular & Pulmonary Systems

Many performance enhancing polymorphisms associated with athletics are expressed in the cardiovascular and pulmonary systems. They affect physiological features such as vasodilatation, inflammatory reactions, and skeletal muscle efficiency. Genotypes associated with the cardiovascular and pulmonary systems can improve recovery, lung capacity, oxygen usage, and overall efficiency while running.

Genes: ACE, HIF1A, NRF1, PPARGCIA, Adrenergic Receptors, & Intronic microsatellite/NO53
