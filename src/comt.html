Nervous System

Catechol O-methyltransferase (COMT)

The gene for the catechol O-methyltransferase enzyme which is one of two enzymes that metabolize catecholemines such as dopamine norepinephrine, and epinephrine which affect cardiac output, and affect opioid activation (1). The methionine on the 158 position of the COMT gene results in an increase of pain sensitivity due to a decrease in opioid system while a valine at that position results in the opposite affect of heightened pain tolerance and an increase in opioid affinity (3).
