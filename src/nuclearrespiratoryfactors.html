Nuclear respiratory factors (NRF1&2) & TFAM

Nuclear respiratory factors are involved in protein expression of mitochondria which can assist athletes in maintaining their endurance and recover after exercise (1,11). NRF-1 and NRF-2 have been researched; it is thought that when NRF1 expression is increased the expression of TFAM promoting Krebs cycle function and respiration (11).
